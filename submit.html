The goal of this webring is to point to game sites that might not get the attention they deserve. That said, this goal isn't mean to be exclusive but provide some kind of curation to keep it managable. If you have a game site not in this list or have any questions contact me!

To get your site added to the list contact me or submit a GitHub pull request which adds the site to our list.

Make sure to add a link back to the Video Game Site Webring too as that's what keeps the webring alive!

What Kinds of Game Sites Can We Include?

  • Game communities
  • News websites
  • Retro websites
  • Console websites
  • Game blogs

What Game Sites Will We Avoid?

  • The very most popular video games sites. If you are IGN, Kotaku, Polygon RPS or the like you don't need the help. Nothing in the top 20.
  • Anything owned by a large media conglomerate.
  • Podcasts
  • YouTube & Twitch Channels
  • Video game sites that have not posted new content recently. If the site has a large amount of historically interesting information we can include it.
  • Steam key reselling websites
  • Loot box/crate websites
  • Site focusing primarily on commerce or buying and selling games

Feel free to submit anything you feel hits our guidelines and check out what sites are already in our list!
